Photo Gallery:National Landmarks

The Gateway Arch is a 630 foot monument in St. Louis, Missouri.
It was built using stainless steel and was built in the form of an inverted, weighted catenary arch. It is also the world's tallest arch.

The Golden Gate Bridge is a red suspension bridge spanning the Golden Gate strait, which is the one-mile wide and three-mile-long channel between San Francisco Bay and the Pacific Ocean.

The Eiffel Tower was constructed to commemorate the centennial of the French Revolution and to demonstrate France's industrial abilities to the world.

The Washington Monument is an obelisk on the National Mall in Washington D.C. It was built to commemorate George Washington, once Commander in Chief of the Continental Army and the first American president.

Mount Rushmore National Memorial is a huge mountain sculpture of four US presidents, which are George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, Theodore "Teddy" Roosevelt, and Abraham Lincoln.
